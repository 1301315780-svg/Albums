DIE LIT

Released on May 11, 2018, Die Lit is the debut studio album by Atlanta rapper Playboi Carti. It arrived as a surprise drop through AWGE and Interscope Records.

The black-and-white artwork, shot by Nick Walker, features Carti flipping into a moshpit while giving double middle fingers. Inspired by vintage punk rock concert photography, it reflects the album's rebellious and raw spirit.
